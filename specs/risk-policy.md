# Risk Policy

Spec version: 1.0.0

## Risk review

Every completed report records material business, financial, valuation, data, and
method risks. Risks must be specific enough to connect a cause, possible effect,
and the evidence or uncertainty that supports them.

## Counter-case requirements

- Include evidence that weakens the main interpretation.
- Identify assumptions whose failure would materially change the result.
- Keep unknowns separate from confirmed negative facts.
- Describe concentration, leverage, liquidity, accounting-quality, and data-age
  risks when applicable.
- Avoid false precision in likelihoods or impacts unsupported by evidence.
- Never describe an outcome as risk-free or guaranteed.

## Escalation boundary

Missing critical data, contradictory provenance, or an unresolved identity must
produce a visible warning and may block a conclusion. Risk review does not place
orders, connect to a brokerage, or perform real-money activity.
