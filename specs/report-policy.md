# Report Policy

Spec version: 1.0.0

## Required structure

A company-research report identifies the subject, demo or real-data status, data
date, generation time, executive summary, company overview, financial analysis,
earnings quality, cash-flow and balance-sheet analysis, basic valuation, positive
and negative evidence, primary risks, assumptions, unknowns, data sources, used
Skills, loaded specs, and research boundary.

## Writing rules

- Cite or reference the durable source records behind material factual claims.
- Separate facts, assumptions, estimates, model outputs, and unknowns.
- Carry provider warnings into the report when they affect interpretation.
- Use measured, conditional language and retain the counter-case.
- Clearly declare fictional demo data and that it is not live or real-time.
- Include enough method detail for a reader to reproduce basic calculations.
- Do not hide missing data behind polished prose.

## Prohibited language and actions

Reports contain no personalized buy/sell recommendation, deterministic price
prediction, guaranteed return, risk-free claim, brokerage connection, order
placement, transaction signing, or funds-transfer instruction.
