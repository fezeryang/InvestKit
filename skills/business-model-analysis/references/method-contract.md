# Business Model Method Contract

Version: 1.0

## Model Components

For each component record revenue model, payer, user, value proposition, pricing basis, channel, contract/delivery pattern, cost base, capital/working-capital intensity, and source IDs.

## Economic-State Ledger

Keep demand, signed order, backlog, delivery, recognition, invoice, receivable, and cash collection separate. Never count one state as another.

## Conditional Metrics

Use recurrence/cohorts for subscriptions, take rate for transactions, backlog conversion for projects, utilization for capacity models, and spread/credit metrics for financial models only when inputs are valid.

## Guards

Do not mix reporting dimensions or bridge changed definitions without evidence. Missing customer or cost data stays unknown. Emit no valuation or investment verdict.
