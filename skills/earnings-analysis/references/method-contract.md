# Earnings Event Method Contract

Version: 1.0

## Baselines

Record actual, pre-release consensus, persisted research estimate, and prior management guidance independently with period, definition, unit, source, and observation/publication time.

## Comparisons

Record absolute and percentage differences only for aligned metrics and valid denominators. Guidance comparison preserves range, midpoint rule, and prior/current status.

## Transcript And Revisions

Transcript observations require `transcript_available: true`, a source ID, and speaker. Revisions record old/new values, method, rationale, assumptions, and affected outputs.

## Guards

Reject post-release expectation vintages, GAAP/adjusted mixing, invented commentary, unsupported causality, and beat/miss language without a pre-event baseline.
