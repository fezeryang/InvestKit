# Earnings Quality Method Contract

Version: 1.0

## Diagnostic Record

Record domain, claim/finding ID, evidence IDs, calculation/method, period, applicability, concern or supported-positive state, alternative explanations, follow-up evidence, confidence rationale, and downstream risk path.

## Required Domains

Cash conversion/accruals, working capital, revenue recognition, repeatability/adjustments, capitalization/reserves/impairments, obligations/related parties, and auditor/control/restatement evidence.

## Guards

- Use average balances and annualization only when explicitly valid.
- Suppress conversion ratios for negative or unstable denominators.
- Separate acquired and organic effects.
- Treat missing footnotes as unknowns.
- Never convert a red-flag count into fraud probability or an investment action.
