# Company Dossier Method Contract

Version: 1.0

## Required Domains

Identity/history, products, segments, customers/channels, geography, management/governance, capital allocation, competition/value chain, regulation, disclosures, risks, and gaps.

## Typed Records

- Facts: claim ID, statement, topic, period, source IDs.
- Attributed claims: speaker/issuer, statement, source IDs, corroboration state.
- Unknowns: gap, attempted evidence, impact, next eligible source.
- Conflicts: competing claim IDs and unresolved reason.

## Completion Guards

Require resolved identity and at least one valid company source. A correction must invalidate or refresh dependent findings. Do not create unit-economics, valuation, or thesis conclusions.
