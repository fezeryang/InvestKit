# Bear-Case Method Contract

Version: 1.0

## Independence Record

Record the frozen thesis checksum, thesis-confirming evidence IDs, independent counterevidence IDs, overlap, and any search gap. Never alter the thesis artifact.

## Required Method Records

- Counter-thesis: bounded statement, horizon, underlying IDs, and evidence status.
- Fragile assumptions: assumption ID, fragility rationale, dependency, counterevidence, and impact.
- Adverse mechanisms: cause, intermediate transmission, affected variable, consequence, timing, and evidence IDs.
- Failure signals: observable metric/event, threshold or condition, window, and expected source.
- Severity/likelihood: labeled estimate, rationale, and material input IDs.
- Bear-case killers: evidence or conditions that would disprove the counter-thesis.

## Completion Guards

Require at least one challenged material assumption, one adverse causal chain, one observable failure signal, and one bear-case killer. Require independent counterevidence or an explicit `insufficient_evidence` gap. Exclude trade construction and action labels.
