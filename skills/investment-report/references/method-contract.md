# Investment Report Assembly Contract

Version: 1.0

## Prerequisite Gate

Require the exact workflow artifact set, completed independent bear case, completed source-verification gate, source register, loaded Skill/spec manifests, and checksums for every frozen input. Preserve valid skipped analytical stages with reasons and missing inputs.

## Required Sections

Render research subject, demo declaration, as-of date, executive summary, company and business model, financial and earnings quality, valuation and comps, earnings/events, thesis and bull/base reasoning, independent bear case, catalysts, positive/negative evidence, risks, assumptions, estimates, unknowns, skipped capabilities, source conflicts/register, used Skills/specs, generation time, and non-advice boundary.

## No-New-Material Gate

Build the allowed union of all input claim, source, fact, assumption, estimate, unknown, risk, and warning IDs. Record emitted IDs and reject any new material statement or number. Write the executive summary only from body findings.

## Completeness

Use `complete`, `complete_with_gaps`, or `failed_validation`. Mandatory source or bear-case failure is always `failed_validation`; permitted disclosed gaps may be `complete_with_gaps`.
